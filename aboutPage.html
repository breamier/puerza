About Puerza

Read

Puerza provides the user a comprehensive strength training workout plan detailing the lifting weights and number of repetitions based on the user’s desired exercise set, as well as their inputted target lifting percentage and one rep max for each exercise in the set. The program also gives a list of recommendations for Isolation Exercises that can be done after the main exercise, should the user wish to do so.

How To Use

Log In / Sign Up

Log In with Email and Password

Log in or sign up with your email and password to create an account and use Puerza's tools for your gym session today.

Strength Training

Enter your 1RPM and Lifting Percentage

Strength training is a type of exercise that focuses on increasing the amount of force a muscle or group of muscles can generate. Puerza can generate a workout based on your input of your Lifting Percentage, choice of workout set, and One Rep Max (1RM) in lbs—the maximum weight you can lift for a single repetition of a specific exercise. Knowing your 1RM helps set the intensity levels for your workouts.

Plyometrics

Choose your Intensity

You can also choose to do Plyometrics for your session today. Plyometrics, known as "jump training" or "plyos," is a form of exercise that focuses on rapid contraction and extension of muscles, aimed at increasing power, speed, and strength. The primary goal of plyometrics is to improve the efficiency of the neuromuscular system, enabling muscles to generate maximum force in the shortest possible time of repetitions. By entering your intensity for your plyometrics workout, Puerza will generate a plyometrics workout plan for you.

Keep track of your Workouts

Workout Generated | Review History

A workout plan will be generated base on your choice of training. Puerza enables users to maintain a history of their each time they generate a workout. Puerza preserves the workout plan and the necessary information. Users can review their fitness journey by referring to this stored information.

Puerza's Tools

Generate Workout Plan

Puerza provides the user a way to customize their workout plans to allow a personalized fitness experience while optimizing the use of their time in the gym by sparing them the arduous task of calculating, and make sure that they can focus on their targeted exercises and structured routine.

Isolation Exercises

When the users choose to do strength training, they will be asked as well if they would like to/not to do Isolation Exercises after choosing the main exercises. Puerza will then recommend one exercise based on the category chosen by the user if they wish to do so.

Additional Information

Both Strength Training and Plyometrics Workout plan displays the required rest time for each exercise that the user must follow. Puerza also displays the specific date and time the user generated a workout plan for either strength training or plyometrics for reference.

History

Puerza enables users to preserve and review their prompted workout plan, which features their selected primary exercise like Strength Training or Plyometrics, the workout set, the inputs such as 1RPM, lifting percentage, or intensity, and the datetime of the generated workout.
